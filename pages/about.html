What We Do

Stem club is a gathering of students that meet regularly in a learning environment to work on inquiry based Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math projects.

Club Meetings

STEM holds monthly meetings on Saturdays from 9:30 A.M. to 11:00 A.M. in Room 405.

With Covid-19's restrictions, meetings have temporarily been moved to Mondays from 3:00 P.M. to 4:00 P.M. on Zoom.

Advisor

Bolsa Grande STEM Club, formerly known as MESA Club, is a campus organization that provides students an opportunity to explore and apply their Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math knowledge and skills beyond their classrooms. We usually meet on Saturday mornings or after school. We have many projects spreading across all areas in the field of Engineering. If you are interested in joining our club and competing with us in the upcoming school year, seek one of the officers and sign up.
