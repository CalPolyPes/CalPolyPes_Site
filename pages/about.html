What We Do

STEM club is the best place at Bolsa Grande High School to explore interest and develop technical skills in the STEM field. Members get the opportunity to compete in engaging Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math based competitions in groups, developing their social and technical skills.

Club Meetings

  • Interest meeting on September 16th in room 325 during lunch!
  • STEM holds bi-weekly meetings on Saturdays from 9:00 A.M. to 11:00 A.M. in Room 405.
  • We may have extra throughout the school year to allow for extra time to work on projects as well.
  • During meetings, we will either be doing STEM activities, working on projects, or hosting informative workshops.
