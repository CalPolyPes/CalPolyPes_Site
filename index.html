What We Do

Bolsa Grande STEM strives to offer students a learning environment and get hands-on experience in STEM fields, be it by constructing engineering projects, coding complex algorithms, or participating in exhilarating and meaningful icebreakers—all with the goal to excel in those fields.

Larger Associations

Every year, STEM provides students opportunities to expand and apply their knowledge through a variety of projects and competitions from different organizations. STEM’s two main competitions, MESA and Sidelines, both require students to design, build, and work in teams to make real-life, practical devices.
